Overview

Purpose

Our purpose with this website is to provide information to prospect clients about our business so we can grow and expand our clientbase. We want to make white water rafting accessible to the general public. We believe the beauties and experiences of this earth are for all to enjoy,and should not be exclusive only to those who can pay top dollar.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Our target customers are parents. We want to attract middle-class families with children between the ages of 6-18 years old.What do they want? Our clients want a safe and affordable family experience while on vacation. What do they except to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? By accessing the website, they will be able to find information on packages and pricing, safety measures and contact information to get in touch with us.
